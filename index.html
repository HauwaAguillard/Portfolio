About Me

About Me.

I started out as a programmer and graduated with a Masters in Computer Science with emphasis on Data Science. Soon I realized that my passion lies in User Experience designs. So I began a self taught process of user experience design and because of my technical background, I was able to quickly adapt to UI/UX tools, and with a background in data science, conducting user experience research and finding patterns came with ease. I find so much fulfilment and joy in mapping out user journey, identifying pain points, and crafting a solution that not only works, but is user centric. One of my client's was so happy with their product, and said "I love how your brain works". Another client said "I love how detailed and well thought out your solutions are." It never gets old. Some tools in my toolkit include HTML, CSS, BOOSTRAP, and JavaScript. I also have experience with Nodejs, EJS, NPM, Mongdb, and Python. My workflow is simple
