About Me

About Me.

I started out as a programmer and graduated with a Masters in Computer Science with emphasis on Data Science. Soon I realized that my passion lies in UX/UI designs. So I began a self taught process of user experience design and because of my technical background, I was able to quickly adapt to UI/UX tools, and with a background in data science, conducting user experience research and finding patterns came with ease. I find so much fulfilment and joy mapping out user journey, identifying pain points, and crafting a solution that not only works, but its user centric. One of my client was so happy with their product, and said "I love how your brainworks". Another client said "I love how detailed and well thought your solutions are." It never gets old. My Workflow

My Workflow.

Research

Perform extensive industry research, document findings, and brainstorm ways to incorporate various aspects to current project.

User flows

Determine complete user path by creating wireframes, storyboards, and journey maps while keeping end-user and business goals in mind.

Design

This is the fun part! Create prototype of application for extensive testing. Then repeat research, user flows, & prototype.
